Unit 5: Probability (Chapter 7)

Day 2 - 7.3, 7.5: Independent and Dependent Events; Compound Events

Objectives:

  • Students will be able to:
  1. Determine whether events are independent or dependent. 
  2. Find the probability of independent or dependent events.
  3. Find the probability of mutually exclusive events.
  4. Find the probability of inclusive events.

  2. Lecture/Power Point: 7.3, 7.5: Independent and Dependent Events; Compound Events including Guided Practice. Students will work individually and with a partner (pair share) on Guided Practice. Lecture and discussion of Guided Practice available on Learn.vcs.net.
